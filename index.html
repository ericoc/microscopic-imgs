Microscopic Moss

12 photos

This moss was gathered in northern Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA on the evening of Tues. May 20, 2025.

The following afternoon, on Wed. May 21, the moss was briefly frozen with liquid nitrogen (LN2) before being left to outgas under vacuum for an hour or two, in order to ensure that it was dry.
